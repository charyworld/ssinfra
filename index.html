Types of Project Phases

Architecture Design

Architects work to balance aesthetics, functionality, and compliance with regulations, transforming the client’s vision into detailed plans and blueprints

Development

Development encompasses the broader process of bringing a building project to fruition, from securing financing and permits to coordinating with various stakeholders. It also includes market analysis and project management to ensure the building meets its intended purpose.

Construction

Construction is where the physical building takes shape. This stage involves site preparation, laying foundations, structural work, and the installation of essential systems.

Maintenance

After construction is complete and the building is operational, maintenance is crucial to preserving the structure's Integrity and functionality. Regular inspections, repairs, and updates are performed to extend the building’s lifespan and ensure it continues to meet safety and usability standards.
